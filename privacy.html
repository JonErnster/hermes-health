Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Hermes Health is a personal, single-user application operated by its sole user and owner. It is not a commercial service, has no other users, and does not sell, share, or transmit personal data to any third party.

What data the app accesses

With the owner's explicit OAuth consent, the app reads health and fitness data from the owner's own Google account (activity, sleep, heart metrics, body measurements, and nutrition, via read-only scopes). It may also read the owner's own lab results and smart-scale measurements from services the owner holds accounts with.

How data is used

Data is used solely to generate personal health summaries and trends for the owner. All processing happens on the owner's personal device.

Storage and retention

Data and access tokens are stored locally on the owner's personal computer, protected by operating-system user permissions. No data is stored on remote servers operated by this app. Data can be deleted at any time by the owner by deleting the local files and revoking access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Google API Services disclosure

Hermes Health's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. The app does not use Google user data for advertising, does not sell it, and does not allow humans other than the owner to read it.
