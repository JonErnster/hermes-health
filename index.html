Hermes Health

A personal longevity dashboard: one private assistant that unifies wearable data (sleep, heart rate, activity), bloodwork, nutrition, body composition, and DNA-informed longevity guidance for a single user.

This is a personal, non-commercial application. It stores health data locally on the owner's device and is not offered as a service to the public.
